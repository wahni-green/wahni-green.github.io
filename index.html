Mobile App

With one of our major client migrating from SAP B1 to ERPNext, the need for a mobile app for their sales team was inevitable.



And it wasn't easy.

With multi-companies, around 70+ sales person and numerous distribution links and retails customers, their demand was huge.

If you have more notes than fit in the console, you can scroll down, but more handily, you can scroll the text up by pressing space bar.

How did we handle it?



Tied items to the company but listed them all together in the app for easy order taking.

Sales Person would take a single order from the customer, upon passing the data to ERPNext, the order gets split based on the item-company mapping.



Added offline capability so that orders can be taken even in absence of internet connectivity.

Added option to generate and download reports from the mobile app itself.

And how did we do all that?

Managed all this with whitelisted functions, reusing majority of standard code and functions! Used standard FF authentication and API Key/Secret.
